3D Orange Reconstruction & Grading

2025 • Dual-Camera Pipeline • Thesis Project

Challenge & Solution

Reconstructing high-fidelity 3D meshes of agricultural produce from dual-camera setups while performing real-time quality grading and surface defect segmentation. Built a Python pipeline using texture baking, dual-camera coordinate mapping, and machine learning models to automatically grade oranges (Premium, Grade A, Grade B, Processing, Reject) in a custom 3D web visualizer.

Capture & Processing Workflow
1 Connect dual cameras; position rotating table with fruit.
2 Launch capture_and_reconstruct.py; enter batch count.
3 Verify feeds show READY; press Spacebar to start.
4 Table rotates 360° for 20s as image pairs capture.
5 Press Enter to process reconstruction & quality classification.
6 Review interactive 3D model, grading label, and defect markers.
Key Innovations
  • 3D reconstruction of 2D images via texture baking & camera mapping
  • Dual camera capture loops synced with automated rotating table
  • Real-time grading and defect detection markers in 3D WebViewer
